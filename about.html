Little about me and what im about

A Mother who did everything she could to make sure all my needs were met. Early in life i would draw on everything, including my test at school. Getting my first Tattoo at the age of 11. Thanks Uncle Joe. R.I.P I was immediatly hooked on the idea that people want a display of what is in their mind they cant create. So i did wht any young guy would do... Started Tattooing all my trailer park boys!!
